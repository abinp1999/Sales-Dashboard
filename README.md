1) Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance & Revenue Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI.
The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, cost analysis, profit performance, customer contribution, and product-level sales.
The goal of this project is to convert raw sales data into meaningful business insights and create a professional BI dashboard for decision-making.

2) Dataset Source
This project uses a sample dataset sourced from a YouTube tutorial for learning and practice purposes.
It is not associated with any real company.

3) Business Questions Addressed
.	Which channel generates the highest revenue?
.	What is the total revenue, cost, and profit performance?
.	Which cities contribute the most sales?
.	Who are the top customers?
.	Which products drive maximum revenue?
.	Is the business operating profitably?

4) Key Insights
.	Distributor and Wholesale channels generate the highest revenue
.	North America contributes the maximum sales
.	Top customers contribute a significant share of revenue
.	Certain products dominate overall sales performance
.	Business revenue exceeds total cost, indicating profitability
